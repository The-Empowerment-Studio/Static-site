Why I Decided to Open This Business

It all started four years ago when I finally found the courage to attend Pole Worx in the city. I was looking for a fun, empowering way to work out—something that was both exciting and gave me a chance to feel sexy, all within a supportive environment. What I discovered there was more than just a workout. It was a community. A space where women came together to have fun, lift each other up, and embrace their inner strength. My confidence grew, and I truly found myself. A huge thank you to Jessica, my amazing hip-hop instructor, who helped bring out the best in me. I will forever be grateful for her classes and the care she took in making sure we all felt seen, loved, and supported.

But there was one thing that made it hard for me to fully commit to this amazing journey: the drive. I live out by Excelsior Springs, and while I loved the classes, the commute was a lot to handle. My friends thought it was cool, but they weren't too keen on making the long drive out to the city either. So, I spent two years grappling with the idea of opening a similar space closer to home—somewhere near the small, rural towns, where women could come together to empower each other, boost their confidence, and embrace their sensuality in a safe, non-judgmental space.

Along the way, I discovered another passion of mine—boudoir photography. It's been a hobby for years, and I realized I could combine both of these passions. So, I took the leap, trusted in myself, and opened a studio that's just for women. A place where you can feel strong, sexy, supported, and most importantly—yourself.

I want this studio to be a space that continually evolves, and that means I'm here to listen to you. I want to hear what you need, what you're looking for, and how I can help create the supportive, empowering environment you deserve. There are big plans for this place, but my vision revolves around YOU—creating a space without judgment, a place where women can thrive, have fun, and support one another every step of the way.

So please, come visit, explore what the studio is all about, and let's build something amazing together. This is a place for women, by women, and I can't wait to see where it takes us.
