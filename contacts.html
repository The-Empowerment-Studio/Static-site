Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to my session?

We provide styling guidance for every session! Generally, wear something that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Avoid busy patterns and bring multiple outfit options. We'll discuss specific recommendations based on your chosen session type.

How long will my session take?

Session lengths vary by package: Confidence Boost (1 hour), Boudoir (2 hours), Branding (1.5 hours), Family (1 hour), Achievement (1.5 hours), and Transformation (multiple sessions). We never rush your experience!

When will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your edited photos within 2-3 weeks of your session via an online gallery. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee if you need them sooner for a special event.

Can I bring someone with me?

Absolutely! You're welcome to bring a friend or family member for support. For boudoir sessions, we recommend limiting guests to maintain the intimate atmosphere, but the choice is always yours.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment options including payment plans. A 50% deposit secures your session date, with the balance due at your session. Contact us to discuss options that work for your budget.

What if I need to reschedule?

Life happens! You can reschedule your session up to 48 hours before your appointment without penalty. Last-minute cancellations may incur a rescheduling fee, but we'll always work with you.
